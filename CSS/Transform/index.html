Transform Properties

  • Scale

    • Scale say size increase ho jata ha

  • Transalte

    • Transalte say horizentaly ar vetcially ap move krwa sktay ha

  • Skew

    • Skew say horizentaly ar vetcially ap tircha mor sktay ha krwa sktay ha

  • Rotate

    • Rotate say ap rotate karwa sktay ha

  • Matrix

    • Matrix 1 shorthand property ha jis may ham ikaty propties ko likhta ha

    transform: matrix(scaleX, skewY, skewX, scaleY, translateX, translateY);

    transform: matrix(1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6);

    • Scale X = 1 (no change)
    • Skew Y = 2 shear vertically
    • Skew X = 3 (shear horizontally)
    • Scale Y = 4
    • Translate X = 5px
    • Translate Y = 6px
